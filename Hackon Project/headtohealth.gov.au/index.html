New to Head to Health?

Head to Health offers multiple ways of searching for digital mental health resources. Watch the video to find out more about how to use the website.

Start exploring resources

Head to Health has more than 500 digital resources to support your wellbeing and mental health. You can start by exploring the resources below or use Search or SAM to find resources that best suit you. Add resources to Your Favourites to save them.

Wanna fight Bipolar Disorders?
 Lets Fight In a Fun Way!

Candy Crush Saga

Candy Crush is committed to players’ fun and relaxation that the opening screen features rotating phrases including “Escape the stress of today”.
Play Game

Hearthstone

Hearthstone is a virtual version of a tabletop card game, and you can play against real opponents or the computer.
Play Game

Pokemon Go

Pokémon Go with simple goals for catching the various Pokémon characters, encourages you to walk outdoors and get some exercise.
Play Game

JigSaw Puzzles

If you love working jigsaw puzzles in real life, you may find that playing a virtual jigsaw puzzle game on your mobile device can act as an effective substitute, especially when you’re away from home.
Play Game

Bejeweled

It’s a classic puzzle game that involves swapping differently shaped virtual “jewels” to form sets of at least three. When you form sets of four or five like types and colors, you’re rewarded with special jewels that can blow up other jewels and result in higher scores.
Play Game

Monument Valley

You’ll be challenged by the ever-changing manipulative realm of peculiar geometry and optical illusions.
Play Game

Mario Kart

So what if you’re not able to drive your car on the road these days? You can drive virtually. This game allows you to whiz through obstacle courses that you never knew existed.
Play Game

Alto's Adentures

You’ll enjoy listening to relaxing music as you jump over hills and other obstacles. The game is loaded with beautiful graphics and exciting gameplay
Play Game

Personal Zen

It’s been clinically proven to help exercise the mind and promote overall wellness. Research has shown that interacting with this game for 5-10 minutes a couple of times a week can bring about significant positive results.
Play Game

Relaxing Puzzler

This easy-to-use meditative puzzle game is another perfect solution for those who are constantly feeling anxious. The beautiful scenes are inspired by modern, thoughtful art styles such as those from Zen Gardening, Sumi inks, and Indian Buddhist art.
Play Game

Clash of Clans

Though this strategy game is jam-packed with action, it just maybe what you need to get your mind out of the corona cloud. Go ahead and join a team of players.
Play Game

Angry Birds

You don’t have to feel angry about being locked indoors anymore. This slingshot adventure game is so entertaining that your worries will simply fly away.
Play Game
